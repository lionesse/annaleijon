A small business owner's thoughts in the Corona crisis

In this blog article, as a small business owner, I share my thoughts in the Corona crisis. I explain why I believe that Sweden's criticized approach in this crisis shows both a beautiful belief in humanity and that Sweden is one of the very few countries which are protecting their citizens' fundamental rights in these challenging times. I also give my input to the "end-date"-debate and explain why we must have a trial-and-error approach to finding a measure which will actually slow the pace at which the virus is spreading. Finally, I state what positive effects might come out of this crisis on the other side. For example, by putting a final end to the status quo of face-time in the office space as well as increasing the actual use and the pace of the digitalization.
